10 The LTC2348 is an 18-bit,low noise 8-channel simultaneous sampling successive
11 approximation register(SAR) ADC with differential,wide common mode range
12 inputs. Operating from a 5V low voltage supply, flexible high voltage supplies,
13 and using the internal reference and buffer, each channel of this SoftSpan ADC
14 can be independently configured on a conversion-by-conversion basis to accept
15 ±10.24V, 0V to 10.24V, ±5.12V,or 0V to 5.12V signals. Individual channels may
16 also be disabled to increase throughput on the remaining channels
17 
18 SPI DATA FORMAT :
19  SoftSpan Configuration Word for Conversion N+1
20 Data In : S23 S22 S21 S20 S19 S18 S17 S16 S15 S14 S13 S12 S11 S10 S9 S8 S7 S6 S5 S4 S3 S2 S1 S0
21

23 SDO0 : D17 D16 D15 D14 D13 D12 D11 D10 D9 D8 D7 D6 D5 D4 D3 D2 D1 D0 C2 C1 C0 SS2 SS1 SS0
24 
25 Sx : SoftSpan Configuration Bits
26 Dx : Data Bits
27 Cx : Channel ID Bits
28 SSx : SoftSpan Configuration Bits of corresponding channel
