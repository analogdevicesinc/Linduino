1 /*!
2 LTC5566: Dual Programmable Gain Downconverting Mixer
3 
4 @verbatim
5 
6 The LTC5566 dual programmable gain downconverting mixer
7 is ideal for diversity and MIMO receivers that require precise
8 gain setting. Each channel incorporates an active mixer and a
9 digital IF VGA with 15.5dB gain control range. The IF gain of
10 each channel is programmed in 0.5dB steps through the SPI.
11 
12 SPI DATA FORMAT (MSB First):
13 
14  Byte #1 Byte #2
15 Data In : RP2 RT2[1] RT2[0] IF2[4] IF2[3] IF2[2] IF2[1] IF2[0] RP1 RT1[1] RT1[0] IF1[4] IF1[3] IF1[2] IF1[1] IF1[0]
16  |---- ATT, TUNE, POWER CONTROL WORD FOR MIXER 2 ---| |---- ATT, TUNE, POWER CONTROL WORD FOR MIXER 1 ---|
17 
18 RPx : Reduced Power Mode Bit
19 RTx : RF Input Tune Control Bits
20 IFx : IF Attenuation Control Bits
21 
22 @endverbatim
